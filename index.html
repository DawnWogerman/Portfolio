About Me

Over the years I have worked in several different industries from customer service, retail and manufacturing. I spent 15 years in the manufacturing industry working for a local promotional products company. I served in many roles there including order entry, product development and 12 years in the I.T. department working on 3rd party application design and development which involved all departments throughout the company with a major focus on the UI for our Sales, Order Entry and and routing/inventory. After the release of my position due to the Covid-19 pandemic I started to focus on my passion of web development and coding.

I'm a very dedicated individual who enjoys working for companies with clear company values and always strive to improve myself and others through my work.

In my spare time I enjoy playing video games, traveling and spending time with my dog, Bean.
